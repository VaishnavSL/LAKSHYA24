About Us

Welcome to Lakshya'24! Lakshya'24 is the annual technical extravaganza hosted by Government Engineering College Kozhikode. Our event spans from May 1st to May 3rd, offering three days filled with innovation, competition, and learning opportunities. We offer engaging coding challenges, exciting robotics exhibitions, insightful workshops and inspiring guest lectures by industry experts.
